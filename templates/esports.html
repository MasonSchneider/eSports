  • Looking to Next Year

    The Rose-Hulman eSports Club got approved for its second year budget. The budget is much larger than the first which means we can make events that bring in more participants as well as create a better expereince at the events we host. We are planning to have a club meeting at the beginning of the academic year to figure out when these events should occur.
