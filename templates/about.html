Rose-Hulman eSports Club Est. December 2013

Purpose

Our club's purpose is to host events such as viewing parties, amateur tournaments, and coaching for the enjoyment of eSports at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology.

Constitution

Our constitution can be found here.

Founding

This club was founded by Mason Schneider, Mark Hein, and Josh Wright on the belief that the Rose-Hulman community is one that loves competitive gaming and that a club was needed to increase the social factor of this type of hobby.
