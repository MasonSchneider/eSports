League of Legends February 22, 2014

Event Results:

Congratulations to team Sad Boys on winning this year's event! Click Here to see the full bracket and here to watch some of the games that were streamed.

About this event:

This event will be a League of Legends tournament featuring a 5v5 tournament as well as a 1v1 tournament. The top three places will receive prizes in both tournaments and players can be registered for both events only once. The 5v5 matches will also be on a twitch stream with the potential of casters.

When?

This event will occur on February 22, 2014. The event will start at 5PM EST, teams are expected to arrive at this time and matches will begin around 5:30PM EST once rules have been explained and teams have been accounted for.

Where?

This event will be hosted in the Myers Presentation Room at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. This room has been reserved and teams will have private rooms for their matches. Competitors are required to be in the Presentation Room at the start of the event for rule coverage. Once event goers report to this room more information will be provided about competition rooms.

5v5 Rules:

Champions and Skins:
All champions and skins are allowed in this tournament.
Items:
All items will be allowed in this tournament.
Winning:
To win a team must destroy the other team's nexus
Map:
Every game shall be played on Summoners Rift.
Bracket:
The bracket will be seeded randomly and every match will be a 1 match series except for the 2 final matches which will be a series of 3.
Pick Order:
The tournament will be draft mode and pick order will be decided on by flipping a coin.
Timing:
All participants have 10 minutes to report for their match once it is possible to be played or they will be disqualified.
Teams:
A maximum of 16 teams may register. If there are not at least 8 teams then riot will not provide prizes.

1v1 Rules:

Champions and Skins:
All champions and skins are allowed in this tournament
Items:
All items are allowed in this tournament
Winning:
The winner will be the first player to get a kill, kill a tower, or get 100 cs.
Map:
Every game will be played on the mid lane of summoners rift with only the two river bushes being allowed.
Bracket:
The bracket will be randomly seeded with every match being a best of one series except for the 2 final matches which shall be best of 3
Picking:
The matches shall be blind pick with annonymous champion selection and summoner spells.
Timing:
All participants have 10 minutes to report for their match once it is possible to be played or they will be disqualified.

Sign-ups will occur during the event.
