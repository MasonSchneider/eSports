Club Mixer May 3, 2014

About this event:

This event is meant to bring out the club members and allow them to play in a less competitive environment. There will be pizza, drinks, prizes, and event an ASUS representitive showing off some cool stuff! Anticipated brackets include Hearthstone, Counterstrike, and Starcraft II.

When?

This event will occur on May 3, 2014. The event will start at 2:30PM EST and games will run until all brackets are completed.

Where?

This event will occur at Rose-Hulman in room O257 with some games being played in the adjacent room O259.

Event Rules:

Competitors:
All competitors must be affiliated with Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology.
Prizes:
All prizes will be raffled randomly with names being placed in the raffle the same number of games they are playing in.
Timing:
All participants are allowed 10 minutes to arrive at their game or they may be disqualified. If a competitor is currently in another game the event will skip the match and return to it as soon as the competitor is available.
Conflicting Brackets:
If a competitor is asked to play in two different games that person should play in the game that has the lower average game time.
Brackets:
The brackets will be made at the event and ran by officials of the game. The brackets will be publicly accessible during and after the event.

Registration will occur during the event.
